Database Schema: Bhao.pk

Users Table: Stores account information (user_id, email, password) for registered users.

Relationship: Connected to the Alerts table via user_id (One User sets Many Alerts).

Alerts Table: Manages user-created price notifications.

Columns: alert_id (PK), target_price, and Foreign Keys linking to Users and Products.

Relationship: Links specific users to specific products they want to track.

Products Table: The central catalog of unique items.

Columns: product_id (PK), title, category.

Relationships:

Linked to Alerts (One Product has Many Alerts).

Linked to Store_Listings (One Product has Many Listings across different stores).

Stores Table: Defines the external vendors (e.g., Daraz, Telemart).

Columns: store_id (PK), name, url.

Relationship: Linked to Store_Listings (One Store offers Many Listings).

Store_Listings Table: Represents a specific product offer from a specific store.

Columns: listing_id (PK), current_price, url.

Role: Acts as the bridge between Products and Stores.

Relationship: Connected to Price_History (One Listing has Many History records).

Price_History Table: Logs price changes over time for analytics.

Columns: history_id (PK), price, date.

Relationship: Linked to Store_Listings to track the price evolution of that specific offer.
